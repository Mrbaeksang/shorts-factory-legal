Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 24, 2026

1. Introduction

Shorts Factory ("the Service") is a personal content creation and distribution tool. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information.

2. Information We Collect

  • OAuth Tokens: We store authentication tokens locally on your device to connect with social media platforms (TikTok, YouTube, Instagram). These tokens are never transmitted to any third-party server.
  • Content: Videos and metadata you create are stored locally on your device and uploaded directly to your social media accounts.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely to:

  • Authenticate with social media platforms on your behalf
  • Upload content to your connected accounts

4. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on your device. We do not operate any remote servers or databases. No personal data is collected, stored, or processed on external servers.

5. Third-Party Services

The Service interacts with the following third-party platforms via their official APIs:

  • TikTok (Content Posting API)
  • YouTube (YouTube Data API v3)
  • Instagram (Meta Graph API)

Your use of these platforms is governed by their respective privacy policies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with any third parties.

7. Data Deletion

You can delete all stored data by removing the application and its associated token files from your device. You can also revoke OAuth access through each platform's account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

The Service is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
